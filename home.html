What's Gender Cell?

Counseling and Support Services

We offer confidential counseling services and support groups for individuals facing gender-based discrimination or harassment. Our trained counselors provide a safe space for students to discuss their concerns and receive guidance and support.

Awareness and Sensitization Programs

The Gender Cell conducts regular workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns to educate the campus community about gender equality, sexual harassment, and inclusivity. These programs are designed to foster a respectful and informed campus culture.

Advocacy and Policy Implementation

The Gender Cell actively collaborates with the institute's administration to advocate for policies that promote gender equality and protect against discrimination. We work towards implementing effective measures and creating a more inclusive campus environment
